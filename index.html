DebitLens

Financial clarity, without bank connections.

DebitLens is a personal finance app designed for people who want awareness and control — without linking bank accounts or sharing data.

You enter what matters to you. DebitLens helps you see patterns, upcoming commitments, and where your money actually goes.

Why DebitLens?

  • No bank connections
  • No ads or tracking
  • Data stays on your device
  • Clear views of income, expenses, and recurring payments

Built for clarity, not automation

Automation can hide problems. DebitLens is deliberately manual, so you stay aware of your finances instead of outsourcing them.
